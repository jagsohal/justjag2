Learning Experience Designer

Human beings respond to experiences and learn from them. Learning Experience Design (LXD) focuses on both content (ID approach) and user experience (UX approach).

You have to understand your audience and their unique needs before you design any course. LXD takes into account the diverse realities of learners.

JustJag is built around principles and practices that expressly ensure that the learning journey is enjoyable, engaging, relevant, and informative. This is the era of the digital user, and we have to meet their digital needs - not to mention our own.

I am Jag - on a mission to help us communicate more effectively and efficiently in this digital world.
