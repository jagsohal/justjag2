Design and Development Process

Project Kick-Off

JustJag will develop a detailed Project Plan that documents both the schedule and milestones for the development of interactive elements, as well as the content and design of the course.

Create Course Prototype

We recommend the development of a prototype for the first module of a course. A prototype provides a fully functional demonstration of the strategies presented in the course. This way, stakeholders have a chance to make changes to the overall look and feel and even the underlying structure of the course.

Develop Storyboards

JustJag will develop the draft and final storyboards for the interactive elements based on the detailed content outlined in the approved course Project Plan. 

Develop Alpha/Beta Courseware

JustJag will build the draft media elements as specified in the storyboards. This includes creating all graphics and animations, programming the interactive exercises, and creating all content screens and tools and links to important reference documents if needed. JustJag will make the changes, conduct our rigorous quality control processes and submit the Beta courseware for pilot testing.

Prepare Final Online Course

JustJag will meet with our customers to review the recommendations contained in the pilot test reports. We will make the changes to the course elements, complete our rigorous quality control tests, and submit them in final form.
