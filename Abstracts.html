JUSTJAG VIDEO ABSTRACTS

Bringing research to life 

Communicate research results to a broader audience with engaging and digestible videos and animations. 

WHY? EXAMPLE

NEW MEDIA

A picture is worth a thousand words..

Researchers have been including charts, drawings, andphotographs in their research articles for years. However, research papers with embedded videos or animations are rarely published let alone shared with others. 

Furthermore, academics papers can lead to some inaccessible language at times alienating their potential audience and therefore potential impacts are misinformed/lost.

Video Abstract + Social media = Impact

Your paper deserves it.

JustJag was tasked to synthesise abstract research data into a digestible video format. This involved collaboration with University lecturers and understanding of the subject matter.

These videos have been viewed thousands of times and informed both adults and young people about some of the issues. A set of digital guidelines were also produced by JustJag alongside the research to go out to the UK and beyond.
